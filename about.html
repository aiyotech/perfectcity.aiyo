The story behind your result

PerfectCity.io is a way for you to discover you ideal city. This project was built for the ChallengePost Open Data Summer Jam. The result of the PerfectCity.io quiz is based on real data carefully curated from Statistics Canada and a few other sources. We worked hard to ensure that the data is the most recent we could find to help give you the best results.
